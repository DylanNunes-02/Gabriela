Chapter Four

Our Story

The Beginning — February, 2025

The day everything changed

We started honours having not spoken much before but it all really kicked off with some chatting during tutorials and from there we just kept on talking.

June, 2025

Our first unofficial date

I remember going out for ice cream with you at MOTS, just me and you. You told me about how you had taken your grans phone when you visited her and had to go all the way back to give it back. We spoke about ourselves and our lives growing up (where I found out how much you disliked your school 😂). I remember thinking how cool you were and how I wanted to know more about you...

September, 2025

Our first official date where I told you how I felt and we became official

I remember coming to pick you up and being so nervous because I didn't know how I would ask you out. I remember the dress you wore and how beautiful you looked. It was such an amazing evening from the dinner at Dopio to the walk around the center and the goofy way I told you I liked you 😋. I remember how happy I was and just how perfect everything was that day.

2025

When I knew

There's no definite moment I can point to where I can say thats when I knew I loved you. It was more of a realization over time. A build up of feelings and a care for you that I had never felt for anyone else before. I knew I loved you when I found myself longing to see you even when I had just left your house. I knew I loved you when I found myself thinking about you in every situation even during the SLR... I think, thinking of you helped with that one a lot 😂.


I knew I loved you when the picture of my future wasn't blurry anymore but was your face as clear as day...

Right now — 2026

Today, and every day after

This chapter is still being written. And I can't wait to fill every page of it with you.
